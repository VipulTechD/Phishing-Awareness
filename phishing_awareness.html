How to Identify Phishing Emails

  • Verify the sender email address carefully.
  • Do not click suspicious links or unexpected attachments.
  • Check for spelling mistakes and urgent language.
  • Never share passwords or MFA codes through email.
  • Report suspicious emails to the IT Security Team immediately.
  • Always verify requests involving payments or sensitive data.
